What is PulseChain?

PulseChain is an innovative Layer 1 blockchain developed to improve on the limitations of Ethereum. PulseChain offers faster block times (around 3 seconds) and significantly lower transaction fees, making DeFi, NFTs, and gaming applications more accessible. It uses a Proof-of-Stake (PoS) consensus mechanism, making it more energy-efficient compared to Ethereum's Proof-of-Work (PoW). By duplicating the entire Ethereum state, PulseChain immediately offers a wide range of assets and dApps. The built-in bridge facilitates seamless movement of assets between Ethereum and PulseChain, promoting interoperability and reducing barriers for developers and users.
