What is PulseChain?

PulseChain is a next-generation Layer 1 blockchain that offers users a faster, more cost-effective alternative to Ethereum. With block times of 3 seconds, it significantly enhances transaction speed compared to Ethereumâ€™s 12-15 second block times. This means users experience a more responsive and seamless interaction, especially with decentralized applications (dApps).

PulseChain's adoption of the Proof-of-Stake (PoS) consensus model drastically reduces its environmental impact compared to Ethereum's former Proof-of-Work (PoW). By duplicating the Ethereum state, PulseChain ensures that every token, NFT, and contract on Ethereum is also available on PulseChain, providing continuity for developers and token holders alike. Its seamless cross-chain bridge allows for easy transfer of assets between PulseChain and Ethereum, making it a versatile and interoperable platform.
